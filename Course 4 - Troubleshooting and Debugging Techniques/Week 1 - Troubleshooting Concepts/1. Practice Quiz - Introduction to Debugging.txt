Practice Quiz: Introduction to Debugging



Q: What is part of the final step when problem solving?

Q: Which tool can you use when debugging to look at library calls made by the software?
A: ltrace

Q: What software tools are used to analyze network traffic to isolate problems? (Check all that apply)
A: tcpdump
A: wireshark



Q: The strace (in Linux) tool allows us to see all of the _____ our program has made.
