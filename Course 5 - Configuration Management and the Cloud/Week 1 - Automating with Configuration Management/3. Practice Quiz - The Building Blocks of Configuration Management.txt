Practice Quiz: The Building Blocks of Configuration Management




Q: How is a declarative language different from a procedural language?

A: A declarative language defines the goal; a procedural language defines the steps to achieve a goal.





Q: Puppet facts are stored in hashes. If we wanted to use a conditional statement to perform a specific action based on a fact value, what symbol must precede the facts variable for the Puppet DSL to recognize it?

A: $





Q: What does it mean that Puppet is stateless?

A: There is no state being kept between runs of the agent.





Q: What does the "test and repair" paradigm mean in practice?

A: We should only take actions when testing determines they need to be done to reach the requested state 





Q; Where, in Puppet syntax, are the attributes of a resource found?

A: Inside the curly braces after the resource type
