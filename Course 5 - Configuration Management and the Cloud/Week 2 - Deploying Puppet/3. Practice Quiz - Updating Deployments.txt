Practice Quiz: Updating Deployments




Q: What is a production environment in Puppet?

A: The parts of the infrastructure where a service is executed, and served to its users.




Q: What is the --noop parameter used for?

A: Simulating manifest evaluation without taking any actions




Q: What do rspec tests do?

A: Check the manifests for specific content




Q: How are canary environments used in testing?

A: As a test environment to detect problems before they reach the production environment




Q: What are efficient ways to check the syntax of the manifest? (Check all that apply)
